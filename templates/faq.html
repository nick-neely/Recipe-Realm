Frequently Asked Questions

To submit a recipe, log into your Recipe Realm account and navigate to the "Add Recipe" section. Fill in the details like the recipe title, ingredients, and steps. Once you've added all the details, click on the "Submit" button. Your recipe will be reviewed and, if approved, will appear on the site.

To rate a recipe, you must be logged into your Recipe Realm account. Navigate to the recipe you'd like to rate and scroll down to the "Rate This Recipe" section. Select the number of stars you'd like to give the recipe, with 1 being the lowest and 5 being the highest. Your rating will be saved and will contribute to the overall rating of the recipe.

Yes, you can edit your recipes after submission. Navigate to the recipe you've submitted and click on the "Edit Recipe" button. You'll be able to update any details of the recipe. Remember to save your changes once you're done editing.

If you wish to delete a recipe you've submitted, navigate to that recipe and click on the "Delete Recipe" button. Confirm your decision in the popup. Please note that once deleted, the recipe cannot be recovered.

Absolutely! Recipe Realm encourages users to share their experiences and feedback on recipes. To leave a comment, navigate to the desired recipe, scroll down to the "Comments" section, type your comment, and click "Submit". Remember to be respectful and constructive in your feedback.

Navigate to your profile page and click on the "Edit Profile" button. In the edit section, you will find an option to upload a new profile picture. Choose the desired image from your device and save the changes.
